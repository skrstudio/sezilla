Search BOT

It Sends searches to Google in a visible browser, finds your target domain, clicks the result and moves to the website. This is the fastest and most guaranteed method to help boost your SERP rankings in your journey to reach the first pages on Google SE.

  1. Add at least one set. Each set contains the destination link and keyword.
  2. Click "Add to list".
  3. Add at least one proxy. Residential proxies are highly recommended.
  4. You can assign a different country for each set. - Add as many sets as needed.
  5. To view the browser, click the "Switch View" button.
  6. Click the Start button (Green Play bullet).

Important Notes:

  • If your website does not already appear on Google Search Engine (SE), this BOT will not be effective.
  • Using a proxy for the Search BOT is mandatory. Use at least 50 proxies.
  • Even free proxies are safe for the Search BOT if they are valid.
